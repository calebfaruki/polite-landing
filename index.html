A New Way to Stay Politically Engaged

Polite is here to bring a new way to stay civically engaged. We are working to create a place to explore political ideas, stay informed, and connect with others. Polite is different from traditional social media where people too often go towards their corners and react with hostility to anything contrary. We are here for people who want to stay engaged and learn about other points of views through constructive discussion.

Our Three Step Process

The Poll – The first step is to understand your own political ideas. Use our poll to navigate your own beliefs and see how much it matches with popular politicians.

The Ballot – How do you compare with your candidates? We will show you people who you can vote for in upcoming elections, and compare them with your ideas.

The Scoop – Engage with the community! The Scoop is our most unique feature. We will show up to date news and editorial, all prioritized by your interests and the polite community. The Scoop allows you to share articles, create discussions, and interact with people with complementary and contrary views.
